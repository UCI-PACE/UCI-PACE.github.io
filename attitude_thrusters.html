Attitude Thrusters!

Welcome!

Attitude Thrusters are workshops that are held roughly once or twice per quarter and cover topics that are relevant to grads at all levels (e.g. research, fellowship/proposal writing, conferences). Attitude Thrusters are open to graduate students at all levels and postdocs in the department. Coming soon: a grad to grad (past first year) peer mentorship component!

Computational Research Tools

Description

The goal of this session was to present research tools that could be helpful to graduate students in our department. Postdoc Zach Hafen presented debugging tools for python in the first half while the second half of the session served as an open space for grads to share the tools most helpful to them.
