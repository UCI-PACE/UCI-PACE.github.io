M. Katy Rodriguez Wimberly

Mentor Bio: Katy Rodriguez Wimberly is a 4th year PhD Candidate who studies passive satellite galaxies to learn more about when and how they stopped forming new stars. Outside of research, she loves hiking, watching too much tv and spending quality time with her hubby, baby girl and her pup.

Three areas of expertise: Time Management, Work-life Balance and Fellowship Writing.

Are you willing to be approached by non-mentee first-year graduate students? Yes

Diversity Statement: I am a non-traditional (older than many of my peers) woman of color (mixed ethnicity) graduate student. I live off campus, am married and have an infant daughter (I was pregnant last academic year). I’m willing to talk to any student who holds similar identities and/or in the case of parenthood wants to hold that identity while in grad school. ☺️
